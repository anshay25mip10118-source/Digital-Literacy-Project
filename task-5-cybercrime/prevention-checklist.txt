Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests
- Always verify the identity of the sender before making payments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Use strong and unique passwords for accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Regularly check bank statements and transaction history
- Do not scan unknown QR codes for payments

Financial/UPI Safety:
- Remember: Receiving money does not require approval in UPI
- Always double-check payment requests before approving

Reporting:
- Report cyber fraud at https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number: 1930 immediately in case of fraud
